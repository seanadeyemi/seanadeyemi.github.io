Otunba Adesiji Mate

Chairman

He is a Retired Auditor of the Federal Ministry of Finance, Lagos. He was a celebrated Auditor. His audit work took him round the States in Nigeria including Abuja. He was part of the group that directed and recommended FESTAC, Lagos houses be sold to the public and also a member of the abandoned properties committee in Rivers and Cross River State after the end of the Biafra war. He is a product of Yaba Technical School now Yaba College of Technology. He is a member of Board of Directors of various companies.

Mr. Kolade Oyegbesan

Director

He is a graduate of Accounting from the University of Lagos. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria [FCA]. A Chartered Accountant with over 20 years of experience in providing financial solutions to corporate institutions, 11 years of which have been in banks. He is an Associate member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria [ACIT] and the Institute of Financial Consultants, Vancouver, Canada. He was the Financial Contoller, RADIX CAPITAL LTD, Lagos. He is currently the Group Treasurer of ABUJA ELECTRICITY DISTIBUTION COMPANY.

Mr. Afolabi Folayan, CIS, ACA

Director (Designate)

He is an expert in Corporate Finance, Securities Trading, Lending and Treasury. He has worked with HLB Z. O. Ososanya & Co, a professional accounting firm (1994 to 1998), was a senior financial analyst at Procter & Gamble Nig. Ltd, (1999 to 2001) and an Executive Director with WSTC Financial Services Ltd, a leading investment Bank and Stockbroking House. He is a member of the Audit Committee of Mansard Insurance Plc (previously GTAssur Plc) and has been involved in various Financial Advisory transactions including Privatization of State Owned Enterprises (SOEs) and Primary Market Issues. He is currently the Managing Director, Securities Africa Financial Ltd, previously Skye Stockbrokers Ltd. Mr. Afolabi Folayan will be joining the board by 1st September, 2016.

Professor Tayo Otubanjo

Director (Designate)

He is an Associate Professor and a Senior Lecturer at Lagos Business School, now Pan African University, Lagos, Nigeria. He is a visiting Research fellow at Warwick Business School, University of Warwick (UK) and a Visiting Scholar at Spears School of Business, Oklahoma State University (USA). He worked for Centre Spread FCB Nigeria as Director for Brand Strategy & Account Planning. Prof. Otubanjo has won several academic awards. He has published and edited books on various topics. He consults in the broad areas of Corporate Brand and Corporate Identity Management, Strategic Corporate Communications, Brand Strategy Development, Brand Leadership Management, Strategic Corporate Reputation Management and also Semiotic Deconstruction of Corporate Advertisements. He is a Brand Strategist. Professor Tayo Otubanjo will be joining the Board by 1st September, 2016.

Mr. Ade Buraimo

Managing Director

An experienced commercial and Investment Banker of over 25 years. He was Managing Director, GTBank, Sierra Leone {Where He Won “Bank of the Year-2010” Award} Managing Director, GTHome {Mortgage Banking Subsidiary, GTBank Plc} Group Head, Commercial Banking, GTBank, Lagos He has the following qualifications: He has BSc {Banking & Finance}, Ogun State University. MBF {Masters Banking & Finance}, University of Lagos, {1996} Associate Chartered Institute of Bankers, ACIB {1988}, Fellow Chartered Institute of Bankers, FCIB {2010}, Associate Chartered Institute for Securities & Investment (ACSI) UK. Associate, Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers (ACS) – Nigeria. Non-Executive Member, Fund Managers Association of Nigeria (FMAN) Chairman Ethics and Regulation He has attended the following Management and Strategy courses: Senior Management Programme {SMP}, Lagos Business School. Advanced Strategic Management {ASM} Program, IMD, Lausanne Switzerland. Achieving Strategy through Business Process Change - Cranfield University School of Management, Bedford, United Kingdom.
