Board of Directors

Otunba Adesiji Mate

Chairman

He is a Retired Auditor of the Federal Ministry of Finance, Lagos. He was a celebrated Auditor. His audit work took him round the States in Nigeria including Abuja. He was part of the group that directed and recommended FESTAC, Lagos houses be sold to the public and also a member of the abandoned properties committee in Rivers and Cross River State after the end of the Biafra war. He is a product of Yaba Technical School now Yaba College of Technology. He is a member of Board of Directors of various companies.

Mr. Kolade Oyegbesan

Director

He is a graduate of Accounting from the University of Lagos. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria [FCA]. A Chartered Accountant with over 20 years of experience in providing financial solutions to corporate institutions, 11 years of which have been in banks. He is an Associate member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria [ACIT] and the Institute of Financial Consultants, Vancouver, Canada. He was the Financial Contoller, RADIX CAPITAL LTD, Lagos. He is currently the Group Treasurer of ABUJA ELECTRICITY DISTIBUTION COMPANY.

Mr. Afolabi Folayan, CIS, ACA

Director (Designate)

He is an expert in Corporate Finance, Securities Trading, Lending and Treasury. He has worked with HLB Z. O. Ososanya & Co, a professional accounting firm (1994 to 1998), was a senior financial analyst at Procter & Gamble Nig. Ltd, (1999 to 2001) and an Executive Director with WSTC Financial Services Ltd, a leading investment Bank and Stockbroking House. He is a member of the Audit Committee of Mansard Insurance Plc (previously GTAssur Plc) and has been involved in various Financial Advisory transactions including Privatization of State Owned Enterprises (SOEs) and Primary Market Issues. He is currently the Managing Director, Securities Africa Financial Ltd, previously Skye Stockbrokers Ltd. Mr. Afolabi Folayan will be joining the board by 1st September, 2016.
