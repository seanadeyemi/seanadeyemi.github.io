Mrs. Tosin Ilesanmi

Chief Operating Officer / Executive Director, Wealth Management

Mrs Ilesanmi is a graduate of Educational Management (B.Ed), University of Ilorin. She also attended University of Lagos, where she obtained a Master Degree in Education [Guidance & Counselling]. She has over a decade of experience in Business Development and Wealth Management. She is an Associate of Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers (ACS). She was formerly the Head, Registrar Unit Greenwich Trust Limited, and has worked in GTI Capital Limited as a Relationship Manager and Business Development Manager. She has attended a number of developmental courses within and outside the country including Project Finance and the Public Private Partnership (PPP) program in New York Institute of Finance, New York City, USA.

Mr. George Imade

Executive Director, Investment Banking Division

Mr. Imade holds Master’s degrees in Business Administration and Managerial Psychology from the University of Benin and University of Ibadan respectively. He is an Associate of Chartered Institute of Stock Brokers (ACS) and a registered Capital Market Operator with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). He has over 17 years hands-on experience in investment banking which include debt and equity capital raising. He was formerly the Managing Director of Wema Asset Management Limited, Macsterlos Consulting and Financial Services Limited. He worked in the Capital Markets Group of Afribank International Limited (Merchant Bankers), Investment Banking Group of Diamond Bank Plc and Oceanic Capital Company Limited. He has handled a number of landmark capital markets, structured finance and Advisory services transactions and has also worked in advisory capacity on state governments’ privatization activities including mandates from the Bureau of Public Enterprises, both locally and across border. He was Associate Director and head Primary Equity Capital Markets of Planet Capital Limited. He has attended a number of developmental courses within and outside the country including Euro Money Training on Securitization, Corporate Finance, amongst others.

Mrs. Aina Oyetunde

Company Secretary & Legal Adviser (Resigned)

B Mrs. Oyetunde is a law graduate with L.L.B (Hons), from Ambrose Alli University, Edo State and B.L from the Nigerian Law School. She also attended Queen Mary, University of London where she obtained an LL.M Master of Laws in Commercial and Corporate Law. She is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association and a Member of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators. She was the Company Secretary & Legal Adviser of WSTC Financial Services Ltd, a leading Investment Bank and Stockbroking House. She has extensive legal experience in capital market, commercial & corporate law, corporate governance and Board management.

Mr. Abimbola Olanrewaju

Head Financial Control

Mr. Olanrewaju is an Assistant Associate and Head, Financial Control, He holds a bachelor degree in Accounting from Obafemi Awolowo University, Nigeria. He is an Associate of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). He possesses good financial and management accounting skills.

Engr. Nojeem Alagbala

Real Estate

Mr. Alagbala is an Assistant Associate. He holds a bachelor degree in Civil Engineering and Master's degree in Structural Engineering from University of Lagos. He has about a decade experience in Real Estate and Structural Engineering where he was involved in building billions worth of properties. He has worked with Jagal Nigeria Limited and IAA Associates as a Civil Engineering Consultant and also worked with Ariosh limited (Oil and Gas Firm) as a Design Engineer. He is a member of Nigeria Society of Engineers (NSE), member of Nigeria Institution of Structural Engineers (NIStruct), member of Nigeria Institution of Civil Engineers (NICE). He is a registered Engineer with COREN.
